What changed in this iteration
Stripping back from the Clerk-style layout to a calmer parent experience.

Removed (too admin-heavy)

  • Dark navy sidebar with sectioned navigation, children quick-switch, account chip
  • Breadcrumbs and current-period indicator in the top bar
  • Multi-card dashboards with KPI strips, right rails, and dense lists
  • Period-by-period grid display on every child card
  • Settings page sub-navigation with anchored sections
  • Table-style records view on the child detail page
  • Many filter chips and search affordances per page

Added (parent-friendly)

  • Lightweight horizontal top nav (Home · My children · Alerts) — 3 destinations, that's it
  • One headline status per child today, in plain language ("Present at school", "Arrived 22 min late")
  • Large, calm child cards with status + 3 simple term numbers + one CTA
  • Big touch targets (48px inputs, 42px buttons) and softer corners (10–18px radii)
  • Single-column forms, no enterprise right rails
  • Simple lists instead of tables — easier to scan
  • Action banner for "needs your attention" — always one obvious next step
